## Why Analytics Matters for PMs

Product Managers make decisions. Good decisions require data. A PM who cannot write SQL to answer their own questions is dependent on data teams for information that should be self-serve. This dependency slows down discovery, delays decisions, and limits the PM's ability to develop independent product intuition.

These projects demonstrate that I can move from a product question to a data answer without intermediaries.

## Analytics Standards

- Every project starts with a business question, not a dataset
- SQL queries are commented to explain the logic, not just the syntax
- Every analysis ends with actionable recommendations, not just findings
- Queries are written to be readable by non-technical stakeholders
