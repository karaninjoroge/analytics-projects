# Telecom Churn Analysis

**Author:** Karani Njoroge
**Date:** June 2026
**Tools:** SQL, Excel
**Domain:** Telecom / Subscription Product

---

## Business Problem

A telecom company is experiencing rising monthly churn. Leadership wants to understand: which customers are most likely to churn, what behaviors predict churn before it happens, and what interventions could reduce it.

As the analyst, my job is to move from this business question to data-backed recommendations.

---

## Dataset

Simulated telecom dataset with columns:
- customer_id, tenure_months, monthly_charge, total_charges
- contract_type (Month-to-Month, One Year, Two Year)
- payment_method, paperless_billing
- internet_service, phone_service, streaming_tv
- tech_support, online_security
- churn (Yes/No)

---

## Analysis Questions

1. What is the overall churn rate?
2. Which contract type has the highest churn rate?
3. Does tenure predict churn?
4. Which services correlate with lower churn?
5. What is the revenue impact of current churn?
